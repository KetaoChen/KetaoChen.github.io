## 4. [A.I. Is Coming for the 2026 Midterms](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/02/20/us/politics/ai-midterms-elections.html)

The NYT reports on AI's growing role in U.S. midterm politics. OpenAI is backing a super PAC called "Leading the Future" with over $100M raised, while Anthropic countered with $20M into its own super PAC. Three political camps are emerging: AI accelerationists, safety-concerned moderates, and those focused on specific harms like deepfakes and AI companions for kids.

**Why it matters:** AI companies are now directly spending big on U.S. elections, marking a new era where AI policy becomes a central political battleground.
